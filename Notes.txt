////////////////////////////////////////////////
          Lesson 3 - Routes & Controllers
///////////////////////////////////////////////

- Routes: These define request end points
- Controllers: These define the logic that happens 
when a route is hit

- All defined routes in the routes directory must be registered 
 in server.js  e.g

        app.use('/api/users', userRoutes) //all routes starting with 'api/users
        will be handled in userRoutes
